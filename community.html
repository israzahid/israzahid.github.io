ByteSized

Co-Founder and Lead

This started out as an idea during quarantine, it made me realize how much our youth lacked technical literacy in a world where its more important than ever and how I was very similar when I was younger. In highschool, there is very little done about technical literacy and I want to be apart of that change. So with my co-founders, we started this organization with a goal to show high school students how technology is for everyone and not just 'super techy people'.
